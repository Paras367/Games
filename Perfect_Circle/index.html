How to Play:

  1. Click and hold the mouse button on the canvas
  2. Draw a circle by moving your mouse in a circular motion
  3. Release the mouse button when you're done
  4. See your score and how close you got to a perfect circle!

Pro Tips:

  • Start from the top and move clockwise for best results
  • Keep your movements smooth and steady
  • Practice makes perfect - try multiple times!
  • Aim for consistency in your circle's radius
